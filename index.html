How to play
  1. Type a company name or ticker and submit a guess.
  2. After each guess, you'll see attribute tiles.
  3. Green means your guess matches the answer in that bucket.
  4. For number-based attributes, ‚ñ≤ means the answer is higher; ‚ñº means lower.
  5. You have 8 guesses per day. The puzzle resets daily at midnight New York time.
